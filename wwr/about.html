History

Founded in 2025, Whitewater Rafting was born from a passion for adventure and a deep respect for nature. A small group of experienced river guides started the company with a focus on safety and customer satisfaction. Over the years, Whitewater Rafting has grown, expanding its operations to include a wider range of rivers and offering tours for all skill levels. They remain committed to environmental sustainability and community involvement, ensuring unforgettable experiences for future generations of adventurers.
